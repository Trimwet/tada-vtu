# Gift Room System - User Guide

## Overview

The Gift Room System allows you to send digital gifts to friends, family, or the public through shareable links. Recipients can secure their spot before creating an account, making it easy to send gifts without knowing email addresses or phone numbers.

## Getting Started

### Prerequisites
- A TADA VTU account with sufficient balance
- Internet connection
- Modern web browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge)

### Accessing Gift Rooms
1. Log into your TADA VTU account
2. Navigate to **Dashboard** → **Send Gift** to create gift rooms
3. Visit **Dashboard** → **Gift Rooms** to manage your sent gifts

## Creating Gift Rooms

### Step 1: Choose Gift Type

**Personal Gift (1 recipient)**
- Perfect for individual gifts
- Capacity: 1 person
- Best for: Birthday gifts, thank you gifts, personal rewards

**Group Gift (2-50 recipients)**
- Send to multiple specific people
- Capacity: 2-50 people
- Best for: Family gifts, team rewards, small group celebrations

**Public Giveaway (up to 1000 recipients)**
- Open to anyone with the link
- Capacity: 1-1000 people
- Best for: Social media giveaways, promotional campaigns, community rewards

### Step 2: Set Capacity
- **Personal**: Automatically set to 1
- **Group**: Choose between 2-50 recipients
- **Public**: Choose up to 1000 recipients

### Step 3: Set Gift Amount
- Minimum: ₦50 per recipient
- Maximum: ₦50,000 per recipient
- Your wallet will be charged: `Amount × Capacity`

### Step 4: Add Personal Message (Optional)
- Maximum 500 characters
- Will be displayed to recipients
- Make it personal and engaging

### Step 5: Set Expiration
- **24 hours**: Quick giveaways
- **48 hours**: Standard (recommended)
- **72 hours**: Extended time
- **1 week**: Maximum duration

### Step 6: Create & Share
1. Review your gift details
2. Click "Create Gift Room"
3. Share the generated link via:
   - WhatsApp, SMS, email
   - Social media platforms
   - Direct messaging
   - QR code (coming soon)

## Receiving Gifts

### For Recipients Without TADA VTU Account

1. **Click the Gift Link**
   - View gift details and sender information
   - See available spots and time remaining

2. **Secure Your Spot**
   - Click "Secure My Spot" to reserve the gift
   - Optionally provide contact info for reminders
   - Your spot is held for 48 hours (personal) or 6 hours (public)

3. **Complete Signup**
   - Click "Sign Up" to create your TADA VTU account
   - Fill in required information
   - Verify your account

4. **Claim Your Gift**
   - After signup, return to the gift link
   - Click "Claim Gift" to add money to your wallet
   - Gift amount is instantly credited

### For Existing TADA VTU Users

1. **Click the Gift Link**
   - View gift details while logged in

2. **Claim Directly**
   - Click "Claim Gift" (no reservation needed)
   - Gift amount is instantly added to your wallet

## Understanding Gift Types

### Personal Gifts
- **Capacity**: 1 recipient
- **Best for**: Individual rewards, thank you gifts
- **Expiration**: 48 hours for room, 48 hours for reservation
- **Privacy**: Most private option

### Group Gifts
- **Capacity**: 2-50 recipients
- **Best for**: Family events, team rewards, small celebrations
- **Expiration**: 48 hours for room, 48 hours for reservation
- **Privacy**: Semi-private (only people with link can join)

### Public Giveaways
- **Capacity**: 1-1000 recipients
- **Best for**: Social media campaigns, promotional events
- **Expiration**: 24 hours for room, 6 hours for reservation
- **Privacy**: Public (anyone with link can join)

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**"Insufficient Balance" Error**
- Check your wallet balance
- Ensure you have enough for: `Gift Amount × Capacity`
- Fund your wallet if needed

**"Gift Room Full" Message**
- All spots have been taken
- Try joining other active gift rooms
- Create your own gift room

**"Gift Room Expired" Error**
- The gift room has passed its expiration time
- Expired rooms cannot be joined
- Unclaimed amounts are refunded to sender

**"Already Have Reservation" Message**
- You've already secured a spot in this room
- Complete your account signup to claim
- Check your email for signup instructions

**"Gift Already Claimed" Error**
- This gift has been claimed by you or someone else
- Check your wallet for the credited amount
- View your gift history for confirmation

### Device Issues

**Multiple Device Access**
- Each device can only reserve one spot per gift room
- Use the same device to complete signup and claim
- Contact support if you need to switch devices

**Browser Issues**
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try a different browser
- Ensure JavaScript is enabled
- Check internet connection

### Account Issues

**Signup Problems**
- Ensure all required fields are filled
- Check email for verification link
- Try a different email address
- Contact support if issues persist

**Login Issues**
- Verify your credentials
- Reset password if needed
- Check for account suspension
- Contact support for assistance

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: How long do I have to claim a gift?**
A: Personal and group gifts: 48 hours after joining. Public giveaways: 6 hours after joining.

**Q: Can I cancel a gift room after creating it?**
A: No, gift rooms cannot be cancelled once created. Unclaimed amounts are automatically refunded when the room expires.

**Q: What happens if no one claims my gift?**
A: Unclaimed amounts are automatically refunded to your wallet when the gift room expires.

**Q: Can I see who claimed my gifts?**
A: You can see the number of people who joined and claimed, but personal information is protected for privacy.

**Q: Is there a limit to how many gift rooms I can create?**
A: You can create up to 10 gift rooms per hour to prevent spam.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What information is collected when I join a gift room?**
A: We collect device fingerprint information to prevent abuse and ensure fair distribution.

**Q: Can I join the same gift room from multiple devices?**
A: No, each device can only reserve one spot per gift room.

**Q: What happens if I don't complete signup after reserving?**
A: Your reservation will expire after the time limit, and the spot becomes available to others.

**Q: Can I change my gift room details after creation?**
A: No, gift room details cannot be modified after creation to ensure fairness.

### Security Questions

**Q: How do you prevent abuse?**
A: We use device fingerprinting, rate limiting, and activity monitoring to prevent abuse.

**Q: Is my personal information safe?**
A: Yes, we follow strict privacy policies and only collect necessary information.

**Q: Can someone steal my gift?**
A: No, gifts are secured using device fingerprinting and account verification.

**Q: What if I suspect fraudulent activity?**
A: Contact our support team immediately with details of the suspicious activity.
