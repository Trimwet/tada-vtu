# TADA VTU - Bank Transfer Deposit Fee Structure (Reduced to ₦25)

## Overview

This document outlines the fee structure for bank transfer deposits on TADA VTU. The hybrid fee model includes VAT adjustments to ensure sustainable operations while keeping costs competitive.

## Fee Structure

| Deposit Amount | Fee Type | Fee Amount | Effective Rate | Net Revenue (After VAT) |
|----------------|----------|------------|----------------|-------------------------|
| ₦100 - ₦4,999 | Flat | ₦25.50 | 0.5% - 25.5% | ₦25.00 |
| ₦5,000+ | Percentage | 2.5% + VAT | 2.5% + 0.15% | ~2.5% |

**Note:** Flutterwave adds their own processing fees (~₦20) on top of these amounts for temporary accounts.

## Rationale

### Fee Reduction

Reduced from ₦30.50 to ₦25.50 to be more competitive:

- **Total user cost**: ₦1,000 → ~₦1,045 (vs ₦1,051 before)
- **More competitive**: 4.5% total vs 5.1% before
- **Still profitable**: ₦25 net revenue after VAT

### Comparison with Competitors

| Platform | Deposit Fee | Total Cost (₦1,000) |
|----------|-------------|---------------------|
| OPay | 1.5% | ₦1,015 |
| PalmPay | Free | ₦1,000 |
| Kuda | Free | ₦1,000 |
| **TADA VTU** | ₦25.50 + FLW | ~₦1,045 |
